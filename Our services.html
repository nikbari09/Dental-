Our Services

At Pushpam Dental, we offer a broad spectrum of services to meet your every need. All ages are welcome, from children
to adults. Below, you'll find menu of our many solution that can help you have a healthier, happier smile.

General Dentistry

If you need a routine examination, cleaning, X-ray, oral
cancer screening or sports mouth guards, our dentist can
suit your requirements. Our range of general dentistry
services will keep your teeth, gums and mouth in great
shape.

 Cosmetic Dentistry

Have you ever wanted to change the look of your smile?
Porcelain veneers, tooth contouring, crown lengthening
and teeth whitening are available to alter how your teeth
appear, giving your self-confidence a boost.

Restorative Dentistry

If your teeth have been damaged due to trauma or aging
you require removal or would like to have a dental implant
placed, our many restorative dentistry solutions may be the
ideal answer. In addition to dental implant restorations,
we offer crowns, bridegs, fillings, Bioclear,inlays and outlays.

 Oral Surgery

Sometimes, it isn't possible to save a tooth from extraction.
In such cases, we can perform a routine extraction. We also
offer wisdom teeth extraction and can place dental
implants to restore your oral health.

Facial Pain & TMD

If you suffer from pain in the ear, jaw or elsewhere in the
face, as well as headaches, we have conservation options to
treat your problem. We'll determine the source of your
problem and recommend the necessary treatment to help
you find relief.

 Pediatrics Dentistry

This dentistry covers all children dental treatments. Like RCT,
extraction, scaling, cement filling , etc. This dentistry
involve education of brushing to child and guidelines about
dental care taken by parents too.

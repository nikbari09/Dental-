About Us

Pushpam Dental offers a range of family dental services from our convenient
location at the Kiwale, Ravet, Dehu Road. Our dentist, Dr. Rahul Bari,
warmly welcome new patients of all ages. Whether you're seeking a
gentle, friendly dentist to provide routine checkups or are hoping to achieve
the smile you've always wanted, or require something in between,
we're here to exceed your expectations.

Why Choose Pushpam Dental?

There may be other dentists in our area, but we'd like to think that there
are some key benefits that set us apart:

> Same-day appointments for immediate attention.

> Saturday appointments for those with busy schedules.

> Late opening hours.

> Emergency care 24 hours a day for patients of record.

> Payment plans available with 20% financing.

> In-network provider with most PPO plans.

> Sedation option available.

> Measures to ensure patient health & safety, including Sterisil water distillation, the Solmetex amalgam separator,
